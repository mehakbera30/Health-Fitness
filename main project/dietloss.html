Weight Loss Diet Plan

A balanced, calorie–controlled diet to help you lose fat safely.

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

• Oats + Skim Milk + Fruits ❘ 300 kcal

• OR 2 Egg Whites + Brown Bread ❘ 250 kcal

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

• Green Tea + 5 Almonds ❘ 80 kcal

Lunch (1:30 PM)

• 1 Roti + Dal + Sabzi + Salad ❘ 350 kcal

• OR Grilled Chicken + Veggies ❘ 400 kcal

Evening Snack (4:30 PM)

• Sprouts Bowl ❘ 150 kcal

Dinner (7:30 PM)

• Soup + Veggies ❘ 200 kcal

• Avoid rice & bread at night

Foods You Should Eat

  • ✔ Oats, dal, vegetables
  • ✔ Fruits (apple, berries, papaya)
  • ✔ Lean protein (chicken, paneer)
  • ✔ Nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • ✔ Green tea, herbal tea

Foods to Avoid

  • ✘ Fried and oily foods
  • ✘ Sugary drinks
  • ✘ Fast food & pastries
  • ✘ White bread & junk snacks
