Diabetic-Friendly Diet Plan

Low glycemic, balanced meals to control blood sugar effectively.

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

• Oats + Chia Seeds + Apple ❘ Low GI ❘ 250 kcal

• OR Besan Chilla + Mint Chutney ❘ 280 kcal

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

• Green Tea + 4 Walnuts ❘ 80 kcal

Lunch (1:30 PM)

• 1–2 Multigrain Rotis + Dal + Sabzi + Salad ❘ 350 kcal

• OR Grilled Chicken + Veggies ❘ 300 kcal

Evening Snack (4:30 PM)

• Roasted Chana / Sprouts ❘ 150 kcal

Dinner (7:30 PM)

• Lauki Soup / Vegetable Soup ❘ 150 kcal

• Avoid rice at night

Foods You Should Eat

  • ✔ Whole grains (oats, brown rice, multigrain roti)
  • ✔ High-fiber foods (vegetables, beans, lentils)
  • ✔ Lean proteins (paneer, tofu, chicken)
  • ✔ Fruits with low GI (apple, berries, guava)
  • ✔ Healthy fats (nuts, seeds, olive oil)

Foods to Avoid

  • ✘ White rice, white bread
  • ✘ Sweets, chocolates, sugary drinks
  • ✘ Processed foods & packaged snacks
  • ✘ Potatoes in excess
  • ✘ Fruit juices (high sugar!)
