Keto Diet Plan

A low-carb, high-fat diet designed to promote rapid fat loss and stable energy.

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

• Scrambled Eggs + Avocado + Black Coffee ❘ 350 kcal

• OR Greek Yogurt + Nuts ❘ 300 kcal

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

• Cheese cubes + Almonds ❘ 150 kcal

Lunch (1:30 PM)

• Grilled Chicken + Broccoli + Butter ❘ 450 kcal

• OR Paneer Bhurji + Salad ❘ 400 kcal

Evening Snack (4:30 PM)

• Bulletproof Coffee ❘ 200 kcal

Dinner (7:30 PM)

• Fish / Paneer + Spinach cooked in ghee ❘ 350 kcal

• Avoid carbs completely at night

Foods You Should Eat

  • ✔ High-fat foods (avocado, paneer, cheese)
  • ✔ Eggs, chicken, fish
  • ✔ Low-carb vegetables (spinach, broccoli, zucchini)
  • ✔ Nuts & seeds
  • ✔ Ghee, butter, olive oil

Foods to Avoid

  • ✘ Rice, roti, bread
  • ✘ Sugar & sweets
  • ✘ High-carb fruits (banana, mango)
  • ✘ Potatoes, corn, peas
  • ✘ Pasta & baked goods
