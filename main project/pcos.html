PCOS / PCOD Friendly Diet Plan

Anti-inflammatory, hormone-balancing meals designed to reduce symptoms naturally.

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

• Oats + Chia Seeds + Banana ❘ High fiber ❘ 300 kcal

• OR Vegetable Upma + Green Tea ❘ 280 kcal

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

• Coconut Water + 4 Almonds ❘ 90 kcal

Lunch (1:30 PM)

• 1–2 Multigrain Rotis + Dal + Veg Sabzi + Salad ❘ 350 kcal

• OR Brown Rice + Rajma / Chole ❘ 400 kcal

Evening Snack (4:30 PM)

• Sprouts / Roasted Chana ❘ 150 kcal

Dinner (7:30 PM)

• Vegetable Soup / Paneer Stir Fry ❘ 200 kcal

• Avoid rice & sugary foods at night

Foods You Should Eat

  • ✔ High fiber foods (oats, vegetables, fruits)
  • ✔ Hormone-balancing foods (flax seeds, chia seeds)
  • ✔ Lean proteins (paneer, tofu, dals)
  • ✔ Anti-inflammatory foods (leafy greens, turmeric, berries)
  • ✔ Healthy fats (nuts, seeds, olive oil)

Foods to Avoid

  • ✘ Sugary foods & desserts
  • ✘ Refined carbs (white bread, pasta)
  • ✘ Fried & oily snacks
  • ✘ Red meat in excess
  • ✘ Soft drinks & packaged juices
