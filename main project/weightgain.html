Weight Gain Diet Plan

A calorie-surplus, protein-rich plan to help you gain weight safely.

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

• Oats + Milk + Peanut Butter ❘ 450 kcal

• OR 3 Whole Eggs + 2 Brown Bread ❘ 400 kcal

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

• Banana Shake / Mango Shake ❘ 300 kcal

• OR Handful Nuts (Almonds, Walnuts) ❘ 150 kcal

Lunch (1:30 PM)

• 2 Rotis + Rice + Dal + Paneer/Chicken + Salad ❘ 600 kcal

• Add 1 tsp desi ghee

Evening Snack (4:30 PM)

• Peanut Chikki or Protein Bar ❘ 200 kcal

• OR Boiled Potato + Chaat ❘ 250 kcal

Dinner (8:00 PM)

• Roti + Veg Sabzi + Paneer/Chicken ❘ 500 kcal

• Add curd for extra calories

Before Bed (10:30 PM)

• Warm milk with honey ❘ 150 kcal

• OR 1 fruit (banana/apple)

Foods You Should Eat for Weight Gain

  • ✔ Bananas, mangoes, dry fruits
  • ✔ Whole milk, ghee, cheese
  • ✔ Potatoes, rice, oats
  • ✔ Paneer, eggs, chicken, fish
  • ✔ Smoothies, shakes, peanut butter

Foods to Avoid

  • ✘ Junk food (causes unhealthy fat gain)
  • ✘ Sugary drinks
  • ✘ Skipping meals
  • ✘ Excess tea/coffee
